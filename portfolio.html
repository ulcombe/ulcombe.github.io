Approach

How the work is delivered

Reproducible by design

Pipelines are built to be reviewable, rerunnable, and defensible across research, publication, and regulated settings.

Biology-first interpretation

Statistical and machine learning results are grounded in disease context, assay limitations, and real translational utility.

Cross-functional communication
